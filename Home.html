About Us

Established in 1967, Red Star F.C. is one of the most recognised Junior Soccer Clubs in Waterford City with two Junior teams and an academy consisting of over 60 players and coaches. Currently playing in Star Park in Ballytuckle. In the coming year as members of our academy graduate, we aim to have teams entered into the Schoolboys Leagues in the U11A and U11B levels. Celebrating its 50th Anniversary this year, this vibrant soccer club is located in the centre of the city adjoining many schools, housing estates and business parks, it is easy accessible from all routes and is situated adjacent to the outer ring road. Membership of this local soccer club is open to all schoolboys and schoolgirls from the ages of 4yrs to 10yrs and adult players, volunteers and parents. There is a Children's Soccer Academy held every Sunday morning for age group 4-10 years and also on Wednesday afternoons,please contact Barry at barryegan1991@hotmail.com for enquiries. The children learn the very basics of soccer and our aim is to foster team work and skills as well as having great fun. There is a also an Astro Turf pitch which can be used and is available to for hire to adult groups and for children's parties, please see: Astro Rental. There is a also an Clubhouse that can be hired for functions with a fully stocked bar and the option of finger food provided by Mary Grimes. Please see: Clubhouse Rental for further information on hosting your function here.

Joining Red Star

Red Star Football Club is open to membership and welcomes children, teenagers 16+, adults, parents and guardians to become part of the team at Red Star. Become a player, a volunteer, help with the teams….. your participation will be appreciated. Membership of the club is open to any individual who may wish to join once that individual is not currently a member of any other club within the season, all unattached players or coaches are free to join at any date within the season.

In the close season, the club will run a series of trials in helping ascertain at what level it is recommended a player should play at, every effort will be made to provide football for every individual, irrespective of their standard.Our Soccer Academy begins at the age of 4 upwards and all are welcome, if you wish to join our club please contact Barry.

Anniversary Dinner

As we celebrate our 50th year of football at Red Star, we are delighted to invite all club members past and present to join us in our celebrations on December 10th 2017 in Star Park, for a day of events. Starting with a blitz for the junior members of the club from 10am until 2pm, followed by a Legends Vs Junior League Legends match at 2:30pm. That night will see the Clubhouse a number of bands, speakers and finger food provided.
We have so valued the support of all of our players, supporters, volunteers and sponsors over the years and would love for you to attend.Tickets are priced at €30 and will be available from Kay, Katie, Johnny or Stewart from next week.
Please feel free to invite anyone you know who has been a part of our club over this time.
