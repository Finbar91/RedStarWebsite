Astroturf Rental

We offer a number of floodlit astro pitches covered in 4G, the best quality synthetic grass to make playing any sport perfect for all weather conditions.
Based in Ballytruckle, Waterford our sports grounds include multiple changing rooms, hot showers, car parking on site and lockers available to store valuables.
Enquire today to avail of prices from as low as €30 per hour.

Our Astro pitches can be used for football, hockey, social and corporate events as well as children’s birthday parties. The pitches can be divided into three separate sections that can be used as a 5- or 7-aside pitch or as one 11-side pitch. We offer discounts for all astro block bookings.

Standard Prices

€60 – Full pitch with lights.
€40 – Half pitch with lights.
€30 - Half pitch with lights. (Student/Unemployed discounted rate)
*Prices are per hour.
