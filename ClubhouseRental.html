Clubhouse Rental

The function room here at Red Star is available to hire free of charge, with a deposit required to secure the booking. Our function rooms are ideal for all your special occasions, whether it is a birthday party, engagement/anniversary party, table quiz, corporate social event or a Christmas party. The function room can cater for up to 120 people.

The Function room is equipped with a wide range of Beers, Ciders, Spirits and other minerals and we do party platters or more substantial food options for you and your party. The room has an integrated music systems and stage, whether you want to bring in your own iPod or a DJ.

Contact us on 088 435688 or Barry for more information or to check for availability.
