Team Information

The Premier team currently play in the Premier Division of the Waterford Junior League, finishing the 2016/17 season as Runners Up to Carrick United F.C. The team is currently managed by Alfie Reynolds, who is assisted by Dom Foley. On last season Alfie has said:
We dropped points to some teams we should of defeated easily, whereas Carrick didn't lack the ability to go and win those close games
