Team Information

The Masters Team is a team entered in the Over 35's league in Waterford City. It is mainly comprised of older players who may not be able for the demands of Premier football but still enjoy a more casual game. Matches are 30 mins each half rather than the normal 45 minutes and is played at a more leisurely level than that of other leagues.

The team is currently being managed by Harry Coleman, who is assisted by Jeffrey Deegan.
